# SEO Content Writer — Examples

## Example 1: Blog Post Creation

**Prompt:**
> Write a how-to blog post targeting the keyword "shopify conversion rate optimization." Target audience is DTC brand owners who are technical enough to implement changes but not developers.

**Expected output:**
- Title: "How to Optimize Your Shopify Conversion Rate: 8 Proven Tactics"
- Meta description (155 chars) with primary keyword
- H1 matching title, H2s for each tactic with secondary keywords
- 1,800-2,200 words, Grade 8 readability
- Practical steps with specific Shopify settings/apps referenced
- FAQ section with 3-4 common questions
- CTA to download a conversion audit checklist

---

## Example 2: Brand Voice Analysis

**Prompt:**
> Analyze these 5 LinkedIn posts and define our brand voice. [posts provided]

**Expected output:**
- Voice personality: e.g., "Authoritative Practitioner — speaks from experience, not theory"
- Tone attributes: e.g., "Direct not aggressive, Opinionated not preachy, Data-backed not academic"
- Vocabulary analysis: technical level, preferred phrases, words to avoid
- Sentence structure patterns: average length, use of fragments, rhetorical questions
- Voice consistency score across the 5 samples
- Documented voice guide for team reference

## Example 3: Social Media Optimization

**Prompt:**
> Adapt this blog post into a LinkedIn post and a Twitter thread. The blog is about how most companies waste 30% of their Google Ads budget on irrelevant search terms.

**Expected output:**

**LinkedIn (250 words):**
- Hook: Bold stat in first line ("Most companies are burning 30% of their ad budget...")
- Body: 3 short paragraphs — the problem, why it happens, what to do
- CTA: "Drop 'audit' in the comments and I'll share our search term cleanup template"
- 3-4 relevant hashtags

**Twitter thread (8 tweets):**
- Tweet 1: Hook with the 30% stat (standalone value)
- Tweets 2-6: One tactic per tweet with specific example
- Tweet 7: Summary framework
- Tweet 8: CTA + "Follow for more paid media tips"

## Example 4: SEO Content Audit

**Prompt:**
> Audit this landing page for SEO. It targets "email marketing platform for ecommerce" but isn't ranking. [URL or content provided]

**Expected output:**
- On-page SEO score with specific findings:
  - "Primary keyword missing from H1 — currently 'Welcome to Our Platform'"
  - "Meta description is 210 chars (too long) and doesn't include target keyword"
  - "No H2 headers — content is one long block"
  - "Zero internal links"
- Readability analysis: current score + target
- Competitor comparison: what top 3 ranking pages do differently
- Prioritized fix list with estimated impact
