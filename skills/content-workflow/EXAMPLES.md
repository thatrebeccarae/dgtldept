# Content Workflow — Examples

## Example 1: Full Pipeline Run

**Prompt:**
> Run the full content workflow on the topic: "Why most marketing teams waste their first 90 days with a new analytics tool."

**Expected output:**

**Stage 1 (Research):** Brief with 5-8 sources covering common analytics implementation mistakes, average time-to-value for marketing analytics tools, and expert opinions on onboarding best practices.

**Stage 2 (Draft):** 1,800-word blog post with:
- Hook: stat about analytics tool churn in year 1
- Body: 4-5 common mistakes with evidence
- Framework: "90-day activation plan"
- CTA: download a template

**Stage 2 (Review):** Editorial scorecard showing Voice: 4/5, Structure: 5/5, SEO: 4/5, Accuracy: 5/5, Readability: 4/5. One revision note: "Hook could be more specific."

**Stage 3 (Distribute):**
- LinkedIn post (250 words with the "90-day" angle)
- Twitter thread (8 tweets, one mistake per tweet)
- 3 email subject lines
- 4 pull quotes

## Example 2: Review an Existing Draft

**Prompt:**
> Review this draft LinkedIn post about Klaviyo's new AI features. [draft provided]

**Expected output:** Editorial review with scores:
- Voice: 3/5 — "Shifts from casual to formal in paragraph 3. Match the conversational tone of paragraphs 1-2 throughout."
- Structure: 4/5 — "Strong hook, but CTA is generic. Replace 'What do you think?' with a specific question."
- Readability: 5/5
- Decision: **Revise** — 2 specific edits listed

## Example 3: Distribution Pack from Finished Article

**Prompt:**
> Create a distribution pack for this published blog post about Google Ads wasted spend. [article provided]

**Expected output:**
- **LinkedIn post:** 220 words, opens with "Most companies are burning 30% of their Google Ads budget on irrelevant search terms." CTA asks for reactions.
- **Twitter thread:** 7 tweets. Hook: the 30% stat. Body: one wasted-spend category per tweet with example. Close: link to full article.
- **Email subject lines:**
  1. "Your Google Ads account is leaking money"
  2. "How to find the 30% waste in your ad budget"
  3. "The search term report your agency isn't showing you"
- **Pull quotes:** 4 excerpts under 280 chars each, covering the key stats and recommendations.
