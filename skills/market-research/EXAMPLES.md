# Market Research — Examples

## Example 1: Full Market Research Report

**Prompt:**
> Generate a comprehensive market research report on the customer data platform (CDP) market. Include market sizing, competitive landscape, technology trends, and strategic recommendations.

**Expected output:** A 50+ page LaTeX-formatted report covering all 11 chapters:
- Executive summary with market size ($4.2B in 2024, projected $12.8B by 2029, 25% CAGR)
- Market definition and segmentation (enterprise vs. mid-market, B2B vs. B2C)
- TAM/SAM/SOM sizing with methodology
- Porter's Five Forces (high rivalry, moderate barriers, low substitution threat)
- Competitive landscape with 10 player profiles and positioning map
- Customer analysis by segment with adoption drivers
- Technology trends (AI/ML integration, real-time processing, composable architecture)
- PESTLE analysis (data privacy regulation as dominant factor)
- SWOT synthesis with scenario analysis
- Prioritized recommendations with implementation roadmap

## Example 2: Competitive Landscape Deep Dive

**Prompt:**
> Analyze the competitive landscape of the e-commerce email marketing space. Focus on Klaviyo, Mailchimp, Omnisend, Drip, and ActiveCampaign. I need positioning maps, feature comparisons, and pricing analysis.

**Expected output:**
- 2x2 positioning map (Price vs. E-commerce Depth)
- Feature comparison matrix (automation, segmentation, analytics, integrations, SMS)
- Pricing analysis table with per-contact costs at different scales
- Market share estimates with sources
- Recent strategic moves (acquisitions, product launches, funding)
- Winner/loser analysis by customer segment
- Strategic implications and recommendations

## Example 3: Market Sizing Exercise

**Prompt:**
> Size the market for AI-powered marketing analytics tools in North America. I need TAM, SAM, and SOM with methodology and assumptions.

**Expected output:**
- TAM calculation: total marketing analytics software market in North America
  - Bottom-up: [number of companies] x [average spend on analytics tools]
  - Top-down: [total marketing tech spend] x [analytics share %]
  - Cross-reference with 2-3 published market reports
- SAM: AI-specific subset, filtered by company size and industry
- SOM: realistic 3-year capture assumption
- Sensitivity analysis with optimistic/pessimistic scenarios
- Key assumptions documented with confidence levels
- Growth driver analysis (what accelerates or decelerates adoption)
