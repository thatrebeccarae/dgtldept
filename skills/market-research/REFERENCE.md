# Market Research — Reference

## Analysis Framework Templates

### TAM/SAM/SOM

```
Total Addressable Market (TAM):
- Definition: [Total global market for the product/service category]
- Calculation: [Number of potential customers] x [Average revenue per customer]
- Value: $[X]B
- Source: [Report name, year]

Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM):
- Definition: [Portion of TAM targetable with current product/channel/geography]
- Constraints: [Geography, segment, channel limitations]
- Calculation: TAM x [% addressable]
- Value: $[X]B

Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM):
- Definition: [Realistic capture within 3-5 years]
- Assumptions: [Market share trajectory, competitive dynamics]
- Calculation: SAM x [Realistic market share %]
- Value: $[X]M
```

## CAGR Calculation

```
CAGR = (End Value / Start Value)^(1 / Number of Years) - 1

Example:
- Market 2022: $4.2B
- Market 2027 (projected): $8.1B
- Period: 5 years
- CAGR = (8.1 / 4.2)^(1/5) - 1 = 14.0%
```

## Data Collection Checklist

- [ ] Market size (current year, at least 2 sources)
- [ ] Market size projections (3-5 year forecast)
- [ ] Growth rate (CAGR, historical + projected)
- [ ] Top 10 players by revenue/market share
- [ ] Recent M&A activity (last 2 years)
- [ ] Funding rounds (last 2 years, top companies)
- [ ] Regulatory developments (current + pending)
- [ ] Technology trends (emerging, adopted, declining)
- [ ] Customer segments (size, growth, behavior)
- [ ] Pricing trends (average, range, model shifts)
- [ ] Geographic breakdown (by region or country)
- [ ] Industry expert quotes (earnings calls, interviews, reports)
